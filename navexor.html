- Connect Brazilian products to the international market in a simple, efficient, and reliable way.

Navexor Global Trade operates as an exporter and trading company, creating opportunities for businesses that wish to start or expand their presence in foreign trade. Our goal is to reduce barriers, simplify processes, and transform Brazil's export potential into consistent international business opportunities.

Analysis & Strategy

Every successful export operation begins with a deep understanding of market dynamics. We assess product viability and identify the most promising international opportunities for our partners.

Our team evaluates regulatory frameworks, tax structures, and competitive landscapes to establish a solid and strategic foundation for entering new markets.

Key Focus Areas

  • Market Intelligence
  • Regulatory Compliance
  • Product Positioning
  • Feasibility Analysis
  • Risk Assessment

Operational Execution

With a defined strategy in place, we move into execution by structuring operations and connecting producers with qualified international buyers.

We act as a strategic bridge, ensuring seamless communication, alignment between all parties, and precise coordination across every stage of the transaction. Our approach is focused on reducing complexity and maximizing operational efficiency.

Logistics & Compliance

At this stage, global trade becomes operational. We oversee the entire logistical chain, from origin to final destination, ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and reliability throughout the process.

Compliance is central to our operations. We closely monitor shipping procedures, documentation, and customs requirements to ensure secure delivery and successful execution for all stakeholders involved.

Phase Deliverables

  • Documentation Management. Complete handling of export licenses, certificates of origin, and all required shipping documentation.
  • Supply Chain Coordination. Strategic coordination of freight, routing, and international transport logistics.
  • Transaction Security. End-to-end oversight ensuring financial integrity and operational reliability across the entire transaction.
